Lasagna Recipe

Description:

BY FAR THE BEST Lasagna you'll ever eat. I've made this many times! The entire family beg for this all the time!!I however add extra seasoning, I simmer for 3hrs and instead of sweet sausage I use real maple syrup. I also use 2lb of ricotta cheese instead of what it calls for because in my opinion extra ricotta is very needed. I also use double the cheese, and add cheddar cheese to the top. I suppose it's now my own recipe but I appreciate the base line. I also use this same sauce recipe for spaghetti with my own special family secrets.

Ingredients:

  • Neat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Preparation:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the Noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
