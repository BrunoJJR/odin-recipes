Chopped Salad with Figs and Blue Cheese

Description:

Sweet figs and sharp blue cheese come together with crunchy romaine for a flavorful end-of-summer side salad.

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Figs
  • Olive Oil
  • Cider Vinegar
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Honey
  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Toasted Almonds
  • Crumbled Blue Cheese
    1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned, 5 to 10 minutes. Drain bacon slices on paper towels, reserving 1/2 teaspoon drippings in the skillet.
    2. Add figs, cut sides down, to the skillet. Cook until browned, about 3 minutes.
    3. Whisk together oil, vinegar, mustard, honey, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Add browned figs, bacon, lettuce, almonds, and blue cheese; toss to combine.
